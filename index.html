About

Hello there! My name is Matt and I currently work for the City of Philadelphia. I am primarily working on the Next Generation 9-1-1 project that involves developing a city-wide verified address dataset for future emergency response and city-wide geocoding purposes. This position offers the unique opportunity being the center of all GIS data management between city departments and how different analysts use spatial data in their day-to-day jobs.

In my free time I love sports, I play hockey, and have quite the taste for craft beers.
